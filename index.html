What is JaduPc?

JaduPC, a Bangladeshi startup initiative creating low cost mini computers and laptops with ARM based single board computers running a custom operating system based on Linux. JaduPC's goal is to connect Bangladeshis with useful and proper knowledge, skill building and education by providing access to digital literacy. JaduPC aims to provide access to computers for those without the privilege of this access.
